Latest Updates

Launched

Yammer now supports Office 365 group naming policiesMay CY2018

Admins will have the ability to set up rules around how an Office 365 group is named and will be able to block certain types of words from being included in a group name.

Yammer: Messaging EnhancementsQ1 CY2018

Yammer will improve the messaging experience through a number of initiatives. These will include GIF integrations, celebrating first posts and typing indicators that will enhance messaging for Yammer.

Yammer Web Part for SharePointQ1 CY2018

Yammer will call Yammer's API and surface conversations directly on SharePoint.

Seen countsQ1 CY2018

Yammer will show view counts of users who read your message.

Move conversationsQ1 CY2018

Yammer will enable users to move threads from one group to another.

Rolling Out

Group resources on mobileJune CY2018

Yammer will bring the right navigation resources inside groups onto the mobile app. This will include the 'Group Actions', 'OFfice 365 Resources, and 'Pinned' tabs.
